## Getting Started

### What is the Venue Owner Portal?

The Venue Owner Portal is your central hub for managing all aspects of your venue on the Eddy's Members platform. From here, you can:

- Manage bookings and reservations
- Set up and manage tables
- Track revenue and analytics
- Scan QR codes for guest entry
- Manage venue images and information
- Handle receipts and payments

### Access Requirements

- An approved venue owner account
- Valid email address
- Internet connection
- Modern web browser

### Step 1: Registration

1. Navigate to the venue owner registration page
2. Fill out the registration form with:
   - Your business email address
   - Business name
   - Contact information
   - Venue details
3. Submit the registration form
4. Wait for account approval (you'll receive an email notification)

2. You'll receive an email notification about approval status

## QR Code Scanner

### Accessing the QR Scanner

1. Click **"QR Scanner"** in the navigation menu
2. Or use the QR Scanner icon in the dashboard
3. The scanner interface will open

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: QR Scanner page/interface]**

### Scanning QR Codes

#### Using Desktop Scanner

1. Ensure camera permissions are granted
2. Click **"Start Scanner"** or **"Scan QR Code"**
3. Position the customer's QR code in front of the camera
4. The scanner will automatically detect and verify
5. Booking details will appear if valid

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Desktop QR scanner with camera view]**

#### Manual Entry (Alternative)

If camera scanning isn't available:

1. Click **"Enter Manually"** or **"Manual Entry"**
2. Enter the booking reference number
3. Or scan the QR code with your phone
4. Click **"Verify"**

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Manual entry form]**

### Verifying Entry

After scanning:

1. Booking details will display:
   - Customer name
   - Booking date and time
   - Table number
   - Number of guests
   - Booking status
2. Verify the information matches
3. Click **"Confirm Entry"** to mark guest as arrived
4. Or **"Reject"** if there's an issue

**[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: QR scan result with booking details]**

### Handling Invalid QR Codes

If a QR code is invalid:

- Check if booking exists
- Verify booking date hasn't passed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: How do I add multiple venues?

A: Contact support to add additional venues to your account. Each venue can be managed separately.

### Q: Can I change my venue information after approval?

A: Yes, you can update most information in Settings. Some changes may require admin approval.

### Q: How often are payouts processed?

A: Payouts are processed according to your selected schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly).

### Q: What happens if a customer doesn't show up?

A: You can mark the booking as "No Show" in the booking details. Refund policies apply based on your settings.

### Q: Can I block certain dates or times?

A: Yes, you can set table status to "Maintenance" or contact support to block specific dates.

### Q: How do I handle refunds?

A: Refunds are processed automatically based on cancellation policies. You can also initiate refunds manually from booking details.
